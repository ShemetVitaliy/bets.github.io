What we do:

All American Home Care Agency has been providing exceptional home care services to families and individuals in need since 2013. In just a few short years, we have become one of the leading home care providers in eastern Pennsylvania.

Our mission is to provide the highest quality home care services to our clients and their families in the comfort of their own homes. We understand that every individual has unique needs, which is why we offer personalized care plans tailored to meet each client's specific requirements. Our compassionate caregivers are experienced and dedicated to providing respectful and dignified care and support.

We offer a wide range of home care services, including home health care, specialty care for individuals with diseases or disabilities, companion care, minor housekeeping services, nursing services, respite care, palliative care, hospice care services, and more. We also work closely with local healthcare providers to ensure that our clients receive the best possible care.

Today, we have offices in Philadelphia, Allentown, Feasterville-Trevose, and Harrisburg, and we continue to expand to better serve our communities.

At All American Home Care Agency, we believe that our clients are part of our extended family. Our goal is to improve their quality of life and create a relationship built on trust and satisfaction.

If you're looking for a rewarding career, we invite you to join our team. We're currently hiring for multiple positions, including direct care workers, certified nursing assistants, patient care coordinators, human resources, sales, and more. We offer competitive salaries, generous PTO packages after your first year of work, 401k, and a friendly working environment. Visit our careers page to find the position that's right for you.
